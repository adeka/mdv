About Us

We are one of New Jersey’s finest providers of high quality, affordable dental care. We specialize in dental implants, veneers, crowns, bridges, oral & gum surgery as well as, laser teeth whitening & bleaching and offer unparalleled dentistry for the quality conscious. We create perfect smiles!

We treat gum disease, tooth decay and perform gum-line alteration procedures. Modern Dental Vision Smiles will perform one of the most pain-free dental treatments in the city. A great cosmetic dentist works from the heart to create a beautiful smile that reflects inner vitality and a perfect smile.
