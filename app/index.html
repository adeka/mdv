  • About Us

Leading provider of dental implants in the tri-state area. Providing all diagnostic services including CAT-scan and implants.

Over 12,000 successful implants to date.

We speak English, Russian, Polish and Ukrainian, and have been serving the eastern european community for over 15 years.

We treat gum disease, tooth decay and perform gum-line alteration procedures.

Modern Dental Vision will perform one of the most pain-free dental treatments in the state.

A great cosmetic dentist works from the heart to create a beautiful smile that reflects inner vitality and a perfect smile.

Our offices are spacious, warm, friendly and equipped with the latest technology in laser dentistry.

Dr. Leonard Vertsman

Graduated from Temple University School of Dentistry. Doctor Vertsman has been an organizer of Modern Dental Vision since its inception.

Gabriel Lerman

Graduated SUNY Binghamton University with a BA degree in Biology. Graduated UMDNJ-Dental School. Completed a one-year general practice residency in Newark Beth Israel Medical Center. Member of American Academy of General Dentistry. Working with Modern Dental Vision since 2008.

Gregory Golder
